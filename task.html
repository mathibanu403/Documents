GREEK PIZZA REMEDIES

Preheat the oven at 425degree F(225 celcious).Grease the pizza pan,Iam using 14 inchpan. Lightly flour roll into a 13 inch circle.Pizza pan build up edges slightly.Lightly greased the rolled dough,this helps making the pizza creip.Spoon a few table spoon of sauce into the center of the pizza and use the back of a spoon to spread it out to within one inch of inches. Spread your toppings bell pepper,mushrooms,sprinkle the cheese.Bake for 15 to 18 minutes depend on the oven,until the golden brown. Slice and serve.By now the cheese should have melted well on the pizza and the base should have brownish and crips.
